Earthquake

Get under the table and protect yourself

If shaken violently by an earthquake, get under a table and grab your legs to protect yourself.
Turn off gas and electricity and evacuate

When the vibration stops, shut off the gas and electricity and evacuate quickly.
Evacuate to a wide area

After leaving the building, move to a wide area such as a playground or park.

Avoid to stairs or pillars in large indoor spaces

If the vibration starts in a building with a lot of space, stay away from stairs or near pillars.

Do not board the elevator

When evacuating from a building, use the stairs instead of the elevator.
