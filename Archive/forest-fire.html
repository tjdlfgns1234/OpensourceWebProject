Forest Fire

Do not smoke and make fires on the mountain

You must not smoke or light a fire on the mountain.
No trash burning around the mountain

You must not burn garbage or rice paddy fields around the mountain.
Early small fires can be extinguished easily

Early small forest fires can be extinguished by beating or covering them with a cloak or tree branch.

Evacuate to a safe area if the fire grows

If a forest fire grows, you must quickly evacuate to a safe area such as a rice field, field, or vacant lot.

When evacuating, spray water and remove gas cylinders

If a forest fire spreads to a residential area, evacuate after spraying water around the house and removing gas cylinders.
