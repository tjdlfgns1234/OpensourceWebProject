Flood

Check weather changes

Residents in areas where flooding is expected frequently check weather changes through broadcasting and the Internet.
Check evacuation sites

Know the evacuation site in advance and evacuate immediately when an evacuation order is issued.
Evacuate to a higher place

In the event of a sudden flood, you must evacuate quickly to a higher place.

Shut off electricity and gas

When evacuating, electricity and gas must be turned off.

No car operation

Cars should not be driven in flooded areas.
